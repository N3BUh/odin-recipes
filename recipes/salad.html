Salad

These salad are a perfect add for bread.

Ingredients

  • 200 g mozzarella
  • 4 big aromatic tomatos
  • 1 onion
  • olive oil
  • balsamic vinegar
  • salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Step
    Cut the tomatos and the mozzarella into slices, the onion into bits.
  2. Step
    Put all together in one bowl and add oil, balsamic vinigar and spices to your liking.
