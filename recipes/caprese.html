Caprese

These caprese ar perfect snacks for every party.

Ingredients

  • 600 g mozarella
  • 30 grape tomatos
  • 30 toothpicks
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Step
    Throw Tomatos and cut pieces of mozarella together in one boil and spice it like you wish.
  2. Step
    Put one piece of mozzarella and one tomato together on each toothpick.
